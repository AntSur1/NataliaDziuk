About

Heyoo!

My name is Natalia Dziuk. I’m a young, studying artist from Poland. I go to art school and enjoy pursuing my passions.

I started my adventure with art some years ago through drawing, but that's not the only thing I do now. I love all kinds of manual work, like painting, crocheting, sewing, and carving. I'm also interested in digital painting. When it comes to traditional techniques, I usually use pencils, ink, fineliners, or acrylic paints. Right now, I’m in the process of experimenting and searching for my own style, so I enjoy trying out different ideas and fantasies.

This website was designed and programmed by Antoni Surmiński to support me in my artistic journey. I hope that everyone who visits gets to know my creative process, finds something that speaks to them, and maybe even takes a piece of it with them. If you’d like to order something from me, feel free to contact me on the contact page.

Enjoy my work and welcome!
